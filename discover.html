Oslo

Oslo, Norway’s vibrant capital, blends modern city life with rich history and stunning nature. Explore world-class museums, the scenic Oslofjord, and a thriving culinary scene. Whether you seek cultural experiences or outdoor adventures, Oslo offers something for everyone.

Trondheim

Trondheim, a city rich in Viking heritage, is home to the stunning Nidaros Cathedral and colorful wharves lining the river. Experience a perfect mix of history, student life, and scenic landscapes, making it an unforgettable stop on your journey through Norway.

Ålesund

Ålesund’s breathtaking Art Nouveau architecture and coastal beauty make it one of Norway’s most picturesque cities. Climb up Aksla viewpoint for stunning panoramic views, explore the charming harbor, and discover Norway’s maritime heritage in this unique seaside destination.

View Accommodations

Geiranger

Geiranger is a fairytale fjord destination, famous for its dramatic waterfalls, steep cliffs, and deep blue waters. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this breathtaking location offers scenic boat tours, hiking trails, and unparalleled natural beauty in the heart of Norway.
